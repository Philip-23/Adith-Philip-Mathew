Selected Projects

Engine Piston

Engine Piston and Crank Shaft Assembly

Jet Engine

Aeroplane Jet Engine

Designed and modeled a conceptual jet engine assembly featuring multi-stage compressors, turbine sections, and an exposed cutaway casing to visualize internal turbomachinery architecture and aerospace propulsion mechanics.

Robotic Arm

Robotic Arm

Designed and simulated a multi-axis robotic arm assembly featuring articulated joints, rotational actuators, and a functional gripper mechanism to demonstrate realistic industrial automation and robotic motion control.

Open Differential

Open Differential Assembly

Designed and modeled a conceptual automotive differential assembly featuring a ring gear, bevel gears, differential carrier, axle shafts, and an exposed cutaway housing to visualize internal power transmission, torque distribution, and differential operating mechanics.

Two Stroke Engine

Two Stroke Petrol Engine Assembly

Designed and modeled a conceptual single-cylinder air-cooled 2-stroke petrol engine with a 59 mm bore and 42 mm stroke, incorporating the piston-cylinder assembly, crankshaft mechanism, cooling fins, and intake, transfer, and exhaust ports to demonstrate internal engine architecture, scavenging processes, and power transmission mechanics.

BTMS

Hybrid Battery Thermal Management System Simulation

Designed and modeled a hybrid dual-medium Battery Thermal Management System (BTMS) featuring a 6-cell staggered cylindrical module layout enclosed within an active flow channel, incorporating localized organic Phase Change Material (PCM) jackets, conjugate heat transfer interfaces, and a forced air-cooling duct to demonstrate transient latent heat storage mechanics, multiphase melting boundary propagation, and system thermal gradient mitigation.

Turbocharger

Turbocharger Assembly

Designed and modeled a conceptual turbocharger assembly featuring a centrifugal compressor, radial inflow turbine, integrated CHRA, and a half cutaway casing to visualize internal airflow paths, rotating components, and forced-induction mechanics.
